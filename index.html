Directions:

  1. Scroll to change brush size.
  2. Press the arrow keys to change your brush color.
  3. Press spacebar to clear the canvas.
  4. Press "R" for a rainbow brush.
  5. When game says switch, draw what's on a new card.
  6. When game says swap, return to a previous card.
  7. Create manmade horrors beyond comprehension.
  8. When ready, click close, look at your first card, then start drawing when it says START
